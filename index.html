Bitcoin (BTC)

Bitcoin is the first and most well-known cryptocurrency, created by an unknown person or group of people using the pseudonym Satoshi Nakamoto in 2009. It is a decentralized digital currency that operates without a central authority or government.

Ethereum

Ethereum is a decentralized, open-source blockchain platform featuring smart contract functionality. It was proposed by Vitalik Buterin in late 2013 and development was crowdfunded in 2014. The platform hosts decentralized applications (dApps) and is powered by its native cryptocurrency, Ether (ETH).

Solana

Solana is a high-performance blockchain platform designed for decentralized applications and cryptocurrencies. It uses a unique combination of Proof of History (PoH) and Proof of Stake (PoS) mechanisms to achieve high throughput and low transaction costs.

Shiba Inu

Shiba Inu is an experimental and community-driven cryptocurrency project inspired by the popular Dogecoin. It gained significant attention due to its impressive price growth and meme-oriented community, which often refers to it as the "Dogecoin killer."
